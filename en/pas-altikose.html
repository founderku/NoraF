What is Stainless Hexagon Bar?

Stainless hexagon bar is solid stainless steel stock with a hexagonal cross-section, commonly used where a flat-sided bar is needed for grip or fastening.

Typical Applications

Stainless hexagon bar is used for fasteners, fittings, and machined parts requiring corrosion resistance.
